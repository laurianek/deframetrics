This top bit is here to add space, so we can see what a graph will look like embedded within a <div> on a page, and make sure it handles resizing etc. properly.
It's quite bit, because I'd expect strip plots to be somewhat wider than they are high, and adding a large top section is one way to achieve this. In the final system, the size of the chart will be controlled by the page layout and so is most likely to fall under Laurieann's domain.
